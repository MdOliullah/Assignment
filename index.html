Introduction

Salman Shah was a Bangladeshi actor & Model. Salman Shah was born on 19 September 1971 in the district of Zakiganj in Sylhet district, and his zodiac sign was Scorpio. His father and mother name was Kamar Uddin Chowdhury and mother Neel Chowdhury. Salman Shah is the eldest son of the family.
